Instructions

Please place your iPhone on a table (or other solid surface).

During the task, you will see two buttons at the bottom of the screen, both like the one below.

Throughout the task, please hold your left index finger on the left button, and your right index finger on the right button.

Each one or two seconds, in each trial, there will be a left arrow (←) or a right arrow (→) displayed on the screen. Whenever an arrow appears, please shortly press down on the correct button: the left button for left arrow, the right button for right arrow.

For a full response, you need to reach 75% pressure on the button. To help you during the following practice round, your current finger pressure is shown in a dynamic bar above each button. (These bars will not appear in the subsequent task.) Don't be afraid to press hard: iPhones are designed to easily record up to the 100% pressure.

To end the response (and let the next trial start), you need to lighten the pressure below 25% on the button. (If you accidentally lift your finger from the screen, it is alright, but the task will pause and a warning message will appear. The task will continue as soon as you put your finger back.)

Start the practice with the Start button below. On the next screen, the arrows will begin to appear as soon as you placed both your fingers on the touch buttons.




For the rest of the task, on some of trials, very shortly after the arrow appears, a "STOP!" message will also appear above the arrow. When this happens, please try your best to stop your response and not press any button on that trial.

It is normal and expected if you will not always be able to successfully stop when a "STOP!" message appears. However, if you continue to try very hard to stop when a "STOP!" message appears, you should be able to stop sometimes.

Please balance the requirement to respond quickly and accurately to the arrows while trying very hard to stop to the "STOP!" message.
